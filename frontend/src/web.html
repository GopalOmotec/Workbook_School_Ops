About IRAIF

IRAIF operates at the intersection of education, innovation, and policy to create a future-ready ecosystem. We empower educators, inspire students, collaborate with governments, and unite stakeholders to drive national impact in AI and robotics education.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To establish India as a global leader in AI, Robotics, and Coding education by 2030, creating an inclusive ecosystem that drives innovation, research, and economic growth.

Our Mission

To transform education through technology by training educators, fostering innovation, building strategic partnerships, and creating opportunities for students across India.

Programs & Initiatives

Teacher Training

Comprehensive NEP-aligned certification programs in AI, Robotics, and Coding, equipping educators with cutting-edge pedagogical skills and technical expertise.

Government Collaboration

Strategic partnerships with education ministries and departments to implement national AI education initiatives and policy frameworks across states.

Innovation Events

National hackathons, bootcamps, and innovation challenges that inspire students and foster entrepreneurship in emerging technologies.

National Ecosystem

IRAIF serves as a unifying platform connecting EdTech companies, academic institutions, research labs, and industry partners to collaborate on creating scalable, quality education solutions in AI and robotics.

Industry Partnerships

Collaborating with leading tech companies to provide real-world learning experiences and career pathways for students.

Research & Development

Supporting cutting-edge research in AI education methodologies and developing open educational resources.

NEP 2020 Alignment

IRAIF's initiatives directly support the National Education Policy 2020 through experiential learning, multidisciplinary approaches, early technology exposure, and strong industry-academia linkages, contributing to Atmanirbhar Bharat and New Bharat Nirman.
