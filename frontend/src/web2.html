International Robotics AI Foundation (IRAIF) is a charitable, non-profit organization advancing AI, Robotics, and Coding education through teacher training, policy collaboration, innovation events, and ecosystem partnerships aligned with NEP 2020.

About IRAIF

IRAIF works at the intersection of education, innovation, and policy to build a future-ready ecosystem. We empower educators, inspire students, collaborate with governments, and unite stakeholders on a common platform for national impact.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To make India a global leader in AI, Robotics, and Coding education, research, and innovation, creating a future-ready workforce for the 21st century.

Mission

  • Train and certify teachers in emerging technologies
  • Support government-led future-ready education initiatives
  • Inspire students through innovation and hackathons
  • Build a unified national ecosystem platform
  • Foster industry-academia collaborations

Programs & Initiatives

Teacher Training

NEP-aligned programs in AI, Robotics, Coding, and experiential pedagogy, helping educators meet the growing demand for AI-skilled professionals.

Government Collaboration

Policy advisory, pilot programs, and public–private–academic partnerships to support national strategies like the 'National AI for All' initiative.

Innovation Events

Innovision, iBuild for Bharat, student hackathons and bootcamps that align with the government's push for AI-driven innovation and entrepreneurship.

Ecosystem Platform

IRAIF brings together EdTech providers, technology companies, institutions, and research labs to collaborate, co-create, and scale quality AI, Robotics, and Coding education, contributing to a robust national AI ecosystem.

Academic Partners

Collaborating with schools, colleges, and universities to implement future-ready curriculum.

Industry Partners

Engaging technology companies to provide real-world insights and resources.

Research Labs

Partnering with research institutions to stay at the forefront of technological innovation.

NEP 2020 & New Bharat Nirman

IRAIF supports experiential and multidisciplinary learning, teacher capacity building, early exposure to AI and coding, and strong academia–industry–research linkages—contributing to Atmanirbhar Bharat and New Bharat Nirman. This aligns with India's goal to implement AI curriculum from Grade 3 by 2026 and establish Centres of Excellence in AI for Education.
